Biscoff Cookie Butter Cookies

Irresistibly delicious cookies for all Biscoff lovers

About This Recipe

These Biscoff cookie butter cookies are meant for all you Biscoff lovers. They are loaded with so much flavor from the cookie butter in the dough, and the melty cookie butter filling. Each bite delivers a double dose of that beloved caramelized speculoos flavor that makes Biscoff so addictive.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Scoop 13 one-teaspoon size balls of Biscoff cookie butter onto a piece of parchment or wax paper and place into the freezer to freeze.
  2. Place butter and ¬Ω cup of Biscoff cookie butter in a medium bowl and mix together until smooth. Add both sugars and mix again until light and fluffy. Add egg and vanilla and mix in.
  3. Add salt, baking soda and flour to the bowl and mix on low, being careful not to over mix. Stop mixing when you still have flour remnants around the bowl.
  4. Scoop the dough into large balls using a 3 tablespoon cookie scoop. Take the frozen cookie butter balls out of the freezer. Push cookie butter balls into the cookie dough, making sure to completely cover with cookie dough.
  5. Roll the dough into balls. Place 6-7 cookies on a baking sheet.
  6. Bake the cookies at 350¬∞F for 11-13 minutes, or until golden brown along the edge. Melt 2 tablespoons of cookie butter in the microwave for 15-20 seconds. Drizzle on baked cookies and sprinkle with some flaked salt. Allow to cool and set.
